About Us

Our Story

POKA was born from a love of K-pop and a desire to create a better way for fans to buy photocards. We understand the thrill of finding that card, the sentimental value they hold, and the challenges collectors face in a global community.

That's why we built POKA - a dedicated platform designed with K-pop fans in mind.
